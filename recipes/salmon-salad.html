Salmon Salad

Description

This is the perfect salad to start the day. Light in calories and has the perfect amount of protein. You can add optional seasonings and dressing of one's choice.

Ingredients

    3 Oz Pre-packaged Smoked Salmon
    1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
    2 Tablespoon of Chopped Onions
    3 Cups of Lettuce
    1 Tablespoon of Lemon Juice
    Pinch of Black Pepper
    Pinch of Salt
    1 Cup of Mushrooms
    1 Cup of Sliced Carrots
    Any optional sauces, dressings, and toppings

Steps

  1. Wash and drain the salmon and cut into desired size.
  2. Place into bowl with the rest of ingredients. Mix well.
  3. Add optional seasonings and dressing (make sure to account for added calories).
  4. Serve immediately for freshness.

Nutrition

    Serving Size: 1 Salad
    Calories: 401
    Protein: 24.4 g
    Carbs: 29.3 g
    Fat: 22.5 g
